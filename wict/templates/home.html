About Women in Clean Tech

An SFU-Surrey based club that works to promote and educate others about sustainable technology and connect women who are passionate about clean technology with each other and those who support them. While the focus of this club is to connect and support the female student body interested in this field, all are welcome to join.

Women in Clean Tech (WiCT) is an all-inclusive club aiming to connect students in STEM, as well as facilitate clean tech initiatives and projects for our club members. Our club objectives are:

  • Support women students in STEM
  • Connect students in STEM through social engagement
  • Provide a platform to explore topics related to the clean technology, student mental health, study tips, etc.
  • Facilitate projects for club members
  • Promote and educate sustainable technology among students

Over the past year, our club has grown exponentially due to our social media presence. We have gained over 800 followers (@wict_SFU) in under a year and accumulated an audience of members from all over Western Canada Universities to California and Jamaica! Having made these unique connections, we have been able to provide our members with unique opportunities and branch out beyond a club and into a community. On the WiCT Instagram, we discuss topics such as student mental health and a work-life balance, interview and job search tips, study tips and general information in the STEM community.
