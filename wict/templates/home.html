About Women in Clean Tech (WiCT)

Women in Clean Tech (WiCT) is an all-inclusive club aiming to connect students in STEM, as well as facilitate clean tech initiatives and projects for our club members.

Our club objectives are:

  • Support and connect women in STEM and allies
  • Provide a social media platform for students to remain on top of topics trending in (clean) tech, to explore topics related to the clean technology, student mental health, study tips, etc.
  • Create technical experience for our members through design teams In the past years, we've run annual design teams that have competed in The James Dyson Award, CSA and NASA and currently in the ASHRAE building design competition. Students gain technical experience, new skills and network with industry professionals.

Over the past year, our club has grown exponentially due to our social media presence. We have gained ~900 followers (@wict_sfu) in under a year and accumulated an audience of members from all over Western Canada Universities to California and Jamaica! Having made these unique connections, we have been able to provide our members with unique opportunities and branch out beyond a club and into a community. On the WiCT Instagram, we cover the hottest topics in tech, career and study tips, the importance of mental health and highlight strong women in STEM in our WiCT Vogue covers.
