WiCT Design Team 2022

ASHRAE 2022 Integrated Sustainable Building Design

Members will work collaboratively on the architectural, mechanical, and electrical design of a pre-determined building, approaching Zero Net Energy.

They will schematically design an energy efficient, sustainable project approaching a Zero Net Energy building with minimized energy demands for HVAC and all other technical systems that could be satisfied with locally available and/or building installed Renewable Energy Sources (RES).

Teams consists of:

Architecture / Structural

Mechanical / HVAC

Electrical / Utilities

WiCT Design Team 2020

2020 James Dyson Award Vert Garden

Vert Garden is a vertical hydroponic farming system with automated technology to optimize plant growth, as well as an accompanying app to control mechanisms.

Our inspiration bloomed from the revolutionary technology of vertical farming and rising financial turmoil due to the effects of COVID-19; as food hoarding became usual and frequent trips to the grocery store increased exposure to the virus.
